# Airport Reference V2 Art Direction

- Pipeline: `tile_mode + layered_tilemap`
- Runtime: Phaser, 16×16 logical tiles, 480×320 camera
- Style: original 16-bit top-down RPG pixel art
- Reference traits: polished gray terrazzo, blue-gray carpet, cool cyan glass light, warm gold luxury storefronts
- Perspective: three-quarter top-down, readable from gameplay camera
- Palette: cool airport gray and navy with restrained cyan and warm gold accents
- Separation: floors remain tile layers; storefronts, kiosks, counters and display islands remain independent transparent props
- Exclusions: no people, faces, existing logos, brand names, legible advertisements or copied product packaging
